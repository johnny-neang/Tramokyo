TWENTY FRIENDS,
ONE FOREST,
THREE NIGHTS.

Tramokyo began in 2019 after six of us decided we were spending too many weekends in cities. We drove up to the cedar hills with a tent and a cast-iron pot. It stuck.

We arrive Friday. We leave Monday. We cook together, sleep outside, and do not check our phones. Come for one year, come for all of them.
